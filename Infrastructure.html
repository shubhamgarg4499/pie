Infra-Structure

At Pie Chemicals, we take pride in our robust and modern infrastructure that serves as the backbone of our adhesive and home care product manufacturing operations. Our facilities include two state-of-the-art factories equipped with advanced machinery and cutting-edge technology, ensuring seamless production processes. These factories collectively offer a production capacity of 7,200+ tons, enabling us to meet the increasing demands of our global clientele with ease and efficiency. Our team comprises 25+ highly skilled and dedicated laborers who bring expertise and precision to every stage of production. Their commitment to excellence ensures that every product meets the highest standards of quality and reliability. With this strong infrastructure, we are well-positioned to cater to the diverse needs of our customers worldwide, delivering exceptional products that exceed expectations.
